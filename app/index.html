Welcome to the Lending Club Dapp

The Lending Club Dapp is a decentralized Ethereum App that you can use to start a lending Club with your friends. All you need to get started is a Ethereum account with some Ether and the Meta Mask Chrome extension. Let me give you an example scenario to show you how it works.

Alice, Bob and Charlie are friends that want to do an investment and do not want to pay high fees to a bank for that. They decide to use the Lending Club Dapp instead to pay 10$ worth of Eth to the smart contract each Month. After everyone has payed the contract a random winner will be decided. First Charlie, then Bob and lastly Alice is payed out. Now for Alice this was not too beneficial be cause she could have also saved the money. But Bob and Charlie were able to make a small investment.

If Dave, Ed, Francise, Gina, Hillary, Isaac, Joe, Klaus and Lennard joined the party, each of them will have a chance for a payout of 120$ each Month. Using randomness makes things more fair. How it works

1. Define a contrat or enter the address of an exsisiting lending Club contract

2. Each participant pays contract using the addreses and amount defined previously

3. Inizialize a payout to a random participant of the contract

After these 3 Steps you can start a new Iteration. The Current Contract Round field will show you how many more rounds to go. After all participants have been paid out you can set new contract details

As of right now sending payments is only possible with MetaMask. (Please not that the plugin can be a bit buggy)
